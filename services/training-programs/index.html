Skills, structured

Training programs that complement the placement.

When an internship needs a layer of structured learning around it — a digital marketing workshop, a hospitality module, a soft-skills bootcamp — we design and deliver short training tracks alongside the on-the-job placement.

What's included

Short, focused, measurable.

Our training programs are built around a clear outcome — a skill demonstrated, a portfolio piece produced, a certificate awarded. They run alongside internships so students leave with both the experience and the structured knowledge.

  • Curriculum tailored to the group's field of study
  • Delivered in our co-working space or partner classroom
  • Mix of theory, workshops and applied projects
  • End-of-program portfolio piece or certificate
  • Trainers with industry experience
  • Runs in parallel with internships and accommodation
