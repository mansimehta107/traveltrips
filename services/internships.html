How we match

Placements that fit the student, not just the role.

Before any matching happens, we read each student's CV, motivation letter and language level. Then we propose the host business that best fits — and brief that business on the student before day one.

  • CV and profile review before placement
  • Pre-arrival briefing for the host business
  • On-site visit by a Travel Trips coordinator in the first week
  • Weekly check-in throughout the placement
  • End-of-placement evaluation and certificate
  • Language-level matching with English support if needed
