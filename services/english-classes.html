Learn while you live it

Bespoke English classes built around the student.

No off-the-shelf curriculum. Each student is assessed on arrival and placed in classes designed for their level, their goals and their stay length — whether that's two weeks or a full semester.

Plan classes → Browse tracks
What's included

Real teachers. Real conversations.

Our English classes are run by certified teachers with years of experience working with international students. Lessons are conversational where possible — the goal is for students to leave more confident speaking English, not just better at filling in worksheets.

  • Placement test on arrival
  • Small groups, levels A1 through C2
  • Conversational, academic or business English tracks
  • Certified teachers with international experience
  • Classroom located near accommodation hubs
  • Optional end-of-programme certificate
Tracks

Pick the track that fits the goal.

Track A

Conversational English

Daily-life vocabulary, role-play, fluency drills. The most-booked track.

Track B

Business English

Emails, meetings, presentations — for students on internships in office environments.

Track C

Academic English

For students planning to continue study in English-speaking universities.

Add-on

Industry-specific vocabulary

Retail, hospitality, tourism, social media — vocabulary matched to the internship.

Levels

A1 through C2

Beginner to advanced — students grouped by level after the placement test.

Format

Small groups

Maximum 10 students per class — everyone gets airtime.

How it works

From arrival to first lesson.

STEP 01

Placement test

Quick assessment on day one to confirm level and learning goals.

STEP 02

Class assignment

Student is placed in the right level group and track.

STEP 03

Regular lessons

Classes run around the internship schedule — no clashes.

STEP 04

Closing certificate

Optional certificate confirming hours completed and level reached.
