Where students stay

Stays that feel like home — not hotels.

From warm Irish host families to private apartments and dedicated teachers' accommodation, every option is vetted and visited by our team. Most students choose host families — and most of them say it was the best part of the trip.

Host families

100+ Irish families who are genuinely glad you came.

Our host families aren't doing this as a side business — they do it because they love welcoming international students. They prepare three meals a day, share their daily life, and often turn into a second family that students stay in touch with for years.

  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner included — special meals welcome
  • Private bedroom, shared family spaces
  • Dietary preferences and allergies respected
  • Daily Irish life — meals, conversations, weekend outings
  • Every family vetted and re-checked before each season
  • Coordinator visit during the first week

How it works

How we match accommodation to students.

STEP 01

Share preferences

Tell us about dietary needs, allergies, gender preferences and any cultural considerations.

STEP 02

We match the family

We pick a host family who fits the profile and brief them on the student in advance.

STEP 03

Warm welcome

Family meets the student on arrival, dinner is ready, room is prepared.

STEP 04

Ongoing check-ins

Coordinator checks in regularly with both student and host throughout the stay.
