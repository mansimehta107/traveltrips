Cultural & academic exchange

Step inside an Irish classroom.

For visiting groups who want more than tourism, we arrange visits to local schools, colleges and training institutions — for cultural exchange, classroom observation or partnership building.

Arrange visits → What's included
What's included

Visits with purpose — not just a tour.

Every school visit is set up with a clear goal: a Q&A session, a joint workshop, a classroom observation, or a leadership conversation with the school principal. We brief both sides ahead of the visit so the time together is actually useful.

  • Visits to primary schools, secondary schools, colleges and training institutions
  • Pre-visit briefing with both schools
  • Classroom observation, Q&A or joint workshop formats
  • Optional language exchange between students
  • Letter of invitation and visit summary on request
  • Coordinator accompanies the group throughout
Visit types

What a school visit can look like.

Classroom

Classroom observation

Watch how Irish or Hungarian classrooms run, learn how teachers structure lessons.

Exchange

Language exchange

Visiting students pair with local students for conversation practice in both languages.

Workshop

Joint workshops

Co-run a short workshop or activity with the host class.

Leadership

Principal meetings

For teachers and school leaders — direct conversations with their counterparts.

Networks

College visits

Tours of further-education colleges and vocational training campuses.

Training

Training institutions

Industry training providers across both cities for vocational-stream groups.

How it works

From request to visit day.

STEP 01

STEP 02

We find the right school

We approach schools in our network and confirm willingness and timing.

STEP 03

Briefing both sides

Visiting and host staff are both prepped so the day runs smoothly.

STEP 04

Visit day

Our coordinator joins the group, handles introductions and keeps to schedule.
