24/7 ground service

First handshake at the arrivals gate.

The Erasmus experience starts the moment a student walks out of customs. A friendly face, a name card, a quick check-in, and a clean ride straight to the host family or hotel.

Book transfers → How it works
What's included

Reliable transfers, any hour, either city.

We watch flight numbers in real time, so a late arrival just means a later pickup — never a missed one. Drivers carry name cards, speak English (Hungarian or basic French/Spanish on request), and stay in contact with our coordinator throughout.

  • Dublin Airport (DUB) and Budapest Airport (BUD) pickups
  • Name-card meet at arrivals — no apps, no scavenger hunt
  • Flight tracking with automatic schedule adjustment
  • Solo, small group or full-bus transfers
  • Return transfer at end of programme included on request
  • Drivers vetted and used regularly by Travel Trips
How it works

From booking to handshake.

STEP 01

Share flight details

Flight numbers, arrival times and full passenger list — even a week before is plenty.

STEP 02

We track the flight

If it's delayed or early, the driver knows. No waiting around for nothing.

STEP 03

Meet at arrivals

Driver with a Travel Trips name card greets the student right outside customs.

STEP 04

Straight to the door

Direct ride to host family, hotel or apartment — and our coordinator knows when you've arrived.
