A place to work

A co-working space built for the programme.

Our co-working space is where training sessions run, where students drop in for Wi-Fi between classes, and where teachers and group leaders work during the day. Quiet desks, meeting rooms, good coffee.

Book the space → Facilities
What's available

Desks, meeting rooms and fast Wi-Fi.

Whether the student needs a quiet desk to wrap up a placement report, or a group leader needs a meeting room for a Friday review, our co-working space has it. It's also the home base for our training programs.

  • Hot-desk seating with reliable, fast Wi-Fi
  • Private meeting rooms by the hour
  • Printing, scanning and a small kitchen
  • Available to students, teachers and group leaders
  • Daily, weekly or program-length access
  • Home of our training programs

How it works

Drop in, or book it for the term.

STEP 01

Tell us the need

Drop-in access, a meeting room, or full-time desks for the duration of the stay.

STEP 02

Pick a plan

Daily, weekly or program-length access — meeting room hours included or à la carte.

STEP 03

Get keys

Students and group leaders are added to the building access list.

STEP 04

Use the space

Drop in anytime during opening hours — the team is around if anything is needed.
