Flagship service

A full Erasmus journey — handled end to end.

Most of our partner schools and universities come to us for one thing: an Erasmus experience where everything is taken care of. Internship, host family, English classes, tours, transfers — one programme, one point of contact.

Plan a programme → How it works
What's included

Every part of the programme, in one package.

We've spent a decade refining what an Erasmus experience should feel like. The result is a programme where students arrive, settle in, work, learn and tour — without ever feeling like they're navigating a foreign system alone.

  • Internship placement matched to the student's field of study
  • Host-family accommodation with breakfast, lunch and dinner
  • Bespoke English classes tailored to each student's level
  • Sightseeing tours of Dublin landmarks and Ireland's iconic destinations
  • Airport transfers at any hour, both arrival and departure
  • Dedicated 24/7 coordinator throughout the stay
  • School visits and cultural exchange sessions on request
How it works

Four steps from first call to farewell.

STEP 01

Brief us

Tell us about your group: size, dates, internship areas, language level and any special requirements.

STEP 02

We design the programme

Within a week we send back a full programme — placements, accommodation, classes, tours and pricing.

STEP 03

Students arrive

Airport pickup, host-family introduction, welcome briefing and English-class kick-off on day one.

STEP 04

We stay with you

Daily coordination, weekend tours, and a 24/7 line for the duration of the stay.
