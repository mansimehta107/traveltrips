Our story

A decade of welcoming students into our cities.

Travel Trips began with a simple idea — that an Erasmus experience is only as good as the people you meet along the way. Ten years later, we've built a network of host families, partner schools and tour operators across Dublin and Budapest that share that belief.

Our mission

Make every Erasmus feel like coming home.

Most of what we do happens behind the scenes — meeting students at the airport, briefing the host family on dietary preferences, syncing internship start dates with English classes. The result, on the student's side, is simple: they arrive, they're welcomed, and they have the time of their life.

We don't outsource the things that matter. The people who run our tours, our transfers and our placements have been with us for years. They know the cities and they know the students.

Irish culture, daily life

Beyond the iconic landmarks, what students remember most is the daily life inside their host family — the meals, the music, the stories, the rain showers.
